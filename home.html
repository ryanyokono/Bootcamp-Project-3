personalized lessons

With Lingo, lessons are tailored to your individual language learning goals, proficiency level, and learning style, based on data collected from your progress and performance. This provides a customized and effective learning experience that adapts to your needs and preferences.

instant feedback

Lingo provides immediate and detailed feedback on your pronunciation, grammar, and vocabulary, helping you identify and correct mistakes as you learn, allowing for a more interactive and engaging learning experience, and helps you improve your language skills more efficiently.

adaptive learning

Lingo adapts to your pace and level of learning, challenging you when you're ready to advance and adjusting the difficulty of exercises to your skill level. Keeping you motivated and engaged, and ensures that you're always learning at a level that's challenging yet achievable.

real-world context

Lingo provides cultural context and real-world language use, helping you understand the nuances of the language and its usage in practical situations. Communicate more effectively with native speakers!
